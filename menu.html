MINI METRO

How to Play

Goal: Transport as many passengers as possible before stations get too overcrowded.

Controls

  • Draw Lines: Click and drag from one station to another to create a new line or extend an existing one.
  • Delete Lines: Right-click on a line segment to delete the entire line.
  • Mobile: Use tap and drag to draw, and double-tap to delete.

Stations

Stations appear in different shapes. Passengers will want to travel to a station of a specific shape.

Trains

Trains will automatically run on your lines. You'll get more trains and other upgrades at the end of each week.

Game Over

If a station has too many waiting passengers for too long, it will become overcrowded and the game will end.
